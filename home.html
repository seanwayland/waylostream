Waylostream

Welcome to WayloStream. A new streaming music website. Users buy credits to stream music. Repeated streams converts to ownership. The artist puts a price on a stream and ownership. Say 1 cent per stream and 100 streams is ownership. Ownership allows user to download or stream forever. Artist controls price of stream and ownership. Better metadata so composers, engineers etc are searchable. A fair way to check out new music and compensate artists. Give artists the opportunity to put a price on streaming music. An alternative to the idea that music is FREE.
