I created this website a couple of years ago as an alternative to other streaming music websites . In the current climate of musicians unable to work I feel like it is more important than ever. Musicians are able to log in and upload music and set their own price on a stream. At this time we are not keeping any percentage of revenue created. Musicians are free to upload music and set their own price on a stream. Musicians please email seanwayland at gmail before uploading music thanks. Stay safe !
